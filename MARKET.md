## Product Overview

**Sprout** is a macOS voice assistant application designed specifically for mind wellbeing support. Unlike generic voice assistants, Sprout focuses on emotional wellness, providing a beautiful, calming interface combined with AI-powered conversations.

## Target Market

### Primary Audience
- **Individuals seeking emotional support** - People looking for a gentle, non-clinical companion for daily emotional check-ins
- **Mindfulness practitioners** - Users interested in meditation, breathing exercises, and emotional awareness
- **Privacy-conscious users** - Those who want AI assistance without cloud-based data collection
- **macOS power users** - People who appreciate well-designed, native macOS applications

### Secondary Audience
- **Developers and tech enthusiasts** - Those interested in local AI, voice technology, and beautiful UI/UX
- **Wellness professionals** - Therapists, coaches, and counselors looking for client tools
- **Students and remote workers** - People managing stress and emotional wellbeing

## Unique Value Propositions

### 1. **Privacy-First AI**
- All processing happens locally via Ollama
- No cloud data collection
- Complete control over your conversations
- Offline-capable

### 2. **Beautiful, Calming Interface**
- Audio-reactive ferrofluid orb visualization
- Minimalist, distraction-free design
- Visual feedback through emoji expressions
- Reduces screen time anxiety

### 3. **Mind Wellbeing Focus**
- Purpose-built for emotional support (not productivity)
- Mood tracking and progress visualization
- Guided breathing and meditation exercises
- Hourly random encouragements

### 4. **Natural Conversation Flow**
- Real-time speech recognition
- Context-aware responses
- Automatic pause detection
- Seamless conversation management

### 5. **Open Source & Customizable**
- Full source code available
- Easy to modify prompts and responses
- Extensible architecture
- Community-driven improvements

## Competitive Landscape

### vs. Generic Voice Assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant)
- **Advantage**: Purpose-built for wellbeing, not productivity
- **Advantage**: Privacy-first, local processing
- **Advantage**: Beautiful, calming interface
- **Advantage**: No data collection or advertising

### vs. Mental Health Apps (Headspace, Calm, BetterHelp)
- **Advantage**: Free and open source
- **Advantage**: No subscription fees
- **Advantage**: Complete privacy
- **Advantage**: Customizable AI responses
- **Advantage**: Native macOS experience

### vs. Chatbots (Replika, Character.AI)
- **Advantage**: Voice-first interaction
- **Advantage**: Beautiful visual experience
- **Advantage**: Local processing
- **Advantage**: Wellbeing-specific features
